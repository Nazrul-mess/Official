About Us

Every great journey begins with a story. Ours is no different. Established in 2016, with a group of students from the Master's program at Kazi Nazrul University. Later, other groups of PhD scholars and project staff joined us. Since then, we have consistently grown our capacity and avenues. Presently, we have a capacity of 22 internal members and one external member. We come from various streams and professions. We live together in a fully democratic environment, focused on enhancing both our intellectual and professional skills to compete in the modern world.
